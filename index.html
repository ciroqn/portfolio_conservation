Our Mission Statement

Founded in 1977, our aim as conservationists has always been to conserve and protect wildlife and their habitats - but, as our world gets increasingly complex, we have adapted our approach to create a robust synergy between local communities and the surrounding environment. We want to create a world in which we no longer see the natural environment as an opportunity for exploitation, but as something to be respected and enjoyed, without any party being adversely affected. We hope you can get on board with our mission!

A message from Samuel Mwangi, CEO

Thank you for taking the time to read our mission. Our task ahead is tough, but the people involved in Amani Conservation have worked tirelessly to keep our operations working effectively and efficiently. And, of course, we couldn't do this without members, donors, and readers - so thank you!
